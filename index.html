EPIC Sprout

Overview

Sprout is an annual Bay Area immersion experience hosted by Northwestern EPIC, open to all Northwestern undergraduates interested in exploring entrepreneurship and what it means for themselves firsthand. During the five-day trip, students have the opportunity to visit the Bay Area's best and brightest companies, ranging from global firms to hot startups. They will also participate in EPIC hosted workshops aimed at familiarizing them with the key aspects of building a startup from the ground up. Companies which have hosted Sprout students in past sessions include GSVlabs, Indiegogo, Particle, Workspan, and Google.

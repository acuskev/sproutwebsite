EPIC Sprout

The Trip

Sprout is Northwestern’s annual Winter Break trip that brings 25 NU students to Silicon Valley for a week of awesome site visits as well as conversations and events with incredible alumni. The trip, a week-long crash-course in entrepreneurship, offers a glimpse into life at innovative and entrepreneurial ventures like Facebook, Indiegogo, or 500 Startups, and alters the ideas and mindset of students who attend the trip for the rest of their lives.

The Experience

Northwestern students dive into the heart and soul of Silicon Valley, meeting and understanding the minds of some of the most talented people in their respective industries. Between the site visits, dinners and Q&As with alums (did we mention utilizing Northwestern’s brand new downtown SF campus), real-world challenges and exploring The Bay, this immersive trip will bring a wisdom and excitement to students that can’t be found anywhere else.
